LAYOUT & FORMATTING

I’ve had the opportunity to format several books over the course of my career.

One of the most notable projects was preparing more than fifty books for print with Fiction Express. These books previously existed as purely digital text, so the team and I first worked to create an InDesign template with the interior design and a Photoshop template with cover and back cover for printing. We did print tests with some copies to check margins, colors, font sizes, etc. and finally applied the templates to the entire collection. Tools I use often for layouts and formatting:

  • InDesign (for print books and image-heavy projects such as travel or cookbooks, magazines...)
  • Calibre (for ebook-first projects)
  • Vellum (occasional simple projects)
