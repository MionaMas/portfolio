FICTION EXPRESS

Fiction Express is an academic tool with the mission of improving students' reading skills through participation. New story chapters are published every week in English, Catalan and Spanish and readers can vote on how they would like the plot to continue. In addition, there are a multitude of activities and a forum where readers can talk to each other and with the authors.

I was part of the design and front-end team. Among other things, I created covers for the books (you can read more about that process here), prepared the books for printing, created landing pages for projects and campaigns associated with Fiction Express (formerly Boolino) such as Re.School, updated social media banners and newsletters, and helped in the continuous process of adding new features and improvements to the website.

We were three girls in the design and front-end team; we provided help to other departments if necessary.
