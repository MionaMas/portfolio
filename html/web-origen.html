ORIGEN DESIGN

This website served as an online wholesale store for Origen Design, who also had two physical stores selling gifts and sustainable clothing. The website can no longer be visited; after several years, one of the two stores was sold and the wholesale department shut down. The owners are looking to simplify their lives as they approach retirement.

As the person in charge of the wholesale and graphic design department, I was who updated the website, designed labels for clothing and sometimes material for social media, I organised buyers' invoices using the Factusol program, photographed the model and uploaded new products to the e-store, in addition to preparing and shipping orders. We were a small team so I helped with various jobs as they came up.

  • Website made with Wordpress
  • Online sales facilitated by the WooCommerce plugin
  • All visitors could see certain pages of the site, such as the Lookbook, a photography section that was updated each season and the blog. However, the store area was private, protected with a personal password and accessible only to confirmed customers.
